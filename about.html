Hi, I'm Nimesh 👋

A front-end mobile engineer intern with NSLHUB (Brane Group).

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering and am currently in my senior year. I dwelled in computer science right from the 12th grade when I programmed a simple calculator using the Tkinter Python toolkit. Since then, I have loved creating things that live on the internet.

Currently, I am focusing on my final year research project. I work with a team of four to perform exploratory data analysis and multivariate time series prediction of air quality. We are trying to gain insightful information and fit an accurate ML model that predicts air quality. We are also researching ways to make this digital information accessible and usable to users for the needful circumstances.

I feel happy to code and develop technologies, and I am more fascinated to understand human-computer interactions. The way humans communicate with technology is a time-honored revolutionary change that impacts society positively and, I wake up every day to learn, witness, and contribute to this discipline.
